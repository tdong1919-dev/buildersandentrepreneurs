Our mission

Replace a handful of disconnected tools with one community that genuinely helps you move forward.

Spend less time searching across fragmented platforms — Discord, LinkedIn, Facebook Groups, Meetup, Eventbrite, Slack — and more time building meaningful relationships and successful companies. This is a community-built platform, and the vision keeps evolving based on the people using it.

The platform

Everything a builder needs, under one roof.

Thirteen connected hubs — each designed to remove friction from the work of building a company.

👤

Member Profiles Live

Show what you're building, your stage, skills, industry, and what you're looking for — plus mentor, investor, podcast, and speaker status.

Explore the directory →
🏢

Business Directory

A searchable directory for startups, small businesses, agencies, nonprofits, freelancers, and service providers — with hiring status and links.

🛍️

Products & Services

A marketplace to promote SaaS, AI tools, consulting, design, courses, and physical products so the community can support one another.

💼

Jobs & Opportunities

A community job board — full-time, contract, freelance, fractional, advisory, and co-founder opportunities.

🤝

Mentor Network

Request a mentor or become one. Match on industry, stage, goals, experience, availability, and office hours.

🆘

HAAALLLPPPPP!

A community help board where members post exactly what they need — a technical co-founder, beta testers, intros, legal, sales, or feedback on an MVP.

🎤

Co-Host & Collaborate

Find someone to co-host an event, run a workshop, organize a meetup, host an AMA, cross-promote, or build a project together.

🎙️

Podcast Hub

Promote your podcast, find guests, volunteer as an expert, and discover local shows searchable by topic.

💰

Funding Hub

A matching system for founders and investors — intake for both sides, feeding an AI agent that ranks compatibility and suggests intros.

📅

Community Calendar

Virtual and in-person events, workshops, meetups, hack nights, demo days, office hours, and pitch competitions.

📚

Resource Library

Templates, playbooks, AI prompts, startup guides, grant and accelerator databases, checklists, SOPs, and recommended software.

🤖

Future AI Agents

Matchmaking for founders, investors, mentors, and podcast guests — plus a community concierge and search assistant.
